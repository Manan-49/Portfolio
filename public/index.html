My Skills

My Expertise

Color Grading &Color Correction

Color correction fixes color imbalances for a natural look, while color grading adds artistic tones to enhance mood and storytelling.

Motion Graphics &Keyframing

Motion graphics involve animated elements like text and icons, while keyframing sets movement points to create smooth transitions.

Reverb & Echo

Reverb adds natural ambiance by simulating sound reflections for more depth, while echo creates distinct delayed repetitions for dramatic effects.

L-Cut & J-Cut

An L-Cut continues audio from the first clip into the upcoming clip, while a J-Cut introduces the next upcoming clip's audio before its video appears.
